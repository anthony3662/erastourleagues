Eras Tour Leagues Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following types of information:

  • Email: We collect email addresses to facilitate communication with our users and provide relevant updates and notifications.
  • Username: We collect usernames to personalize the user experience within our app.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Communication: We may use email addresses to send important notices, updates, and promotional materials related to Eras Tour Leagues. Users can opt-out of receiving marketing communications at any time.
  • User Experience: Usernames are used to personalize and enhance the user experience within our app.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We store user information using a secure MongoDB Atlas cloud-hosted database. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or transfer user information to third parties. Eras Tour Leagues is a fan project that is monetized solely through donations, and we do not use user information for any commercial purposes.

5. Prize Acceptance

In the event that you accept a prize offered by Eras Tour Leagues, we may require you to provide additional personal information. This information may be necessary to deliver the prize to you and to ensure compliance with our terms of use. We will handle and protect this additional personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6. Account Closure

If you wish to close your Eras Tour Leagues account and have your personal information deleted from our systems, please email us at erastourleagues@gmail.com. We will process your request and delete your personal information within 14 days of receiving your request.

7. Use of Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience and provide certain features and functionalities. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize you as a user. We only use cookies that are necessary for user authentication and logging into our app. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for the login process.

8. Children's Privacy

Eras Tour Leagues is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at erastourleagues@gmail.com, and we will promptly delete the information.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at erastourleagues@gmail.com.
